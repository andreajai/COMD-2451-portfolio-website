Hola ¿Qué tal? Bienvenidx

My name is Andrea Jaime (pronounced Hi-Meh). I was born and raised in Peru until I was 6 years old, in a small neighborhood in Lima called Naranjal (which loosely translates to "Orange")! I am currently based in Brooklyn, NY.

I am a student at City Tech - CUNY, studying Communication Design with a concentration in Advertising and Graphic Design. I am working on strengthening my skills by conceptualizing ideas and creating dynamic, compelling visuals to tell a story. I am familiar with most Adobe programs, but *Me Pongo Las Pilas* with InDesign!

I'm eager to immerse myself in a collabrating and uplifting environment with other creatives who are just as passionate about art and design as I am! I enjoy exploring design from a psychological perspective.

When I'm not designing, photographing, or collaging, I'm attempting to make delicious home-cooked meals, reading, riding my bike, chatting in Spanglish with friends and family, or watching "Friends" on repeat.
